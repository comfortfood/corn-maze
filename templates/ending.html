Sweet Berry Farm

Home of the real-life four acre Texas-shaped Corn Maze.

Also they have Berry Picking, a Barrel Train, Goat Feeding, Sand Art, Scarecrow Stuffing, the Berry Bounce, and a Pumpkin Patch!

Texas Archive of the Moving Image

They have a huge archive of home movies, amateur films, ads, local television productions, as well as Hollywood and internationally produced moving images of Texas available online.

Nixta Taqueria

Located in Austin, Texas. A favorite place that makes amazing tacos served on fresh corn tortillas (corn like the corn maze) made from scratch daily.
